Design — Personal security apparatus

v1
Tinkercad

A compact personal security keychain designed for everyday carry, this simple apparatus combines a loud whistle to quickly draw attention in an emergency with a cute cat-shaped safety keychain that’s easy to grip and keep close at hand. Lightweight and convenient, it clips securely to keys and can be hung on bookbags, purses, backpacks, or belt loops so it’s always within reach. Ideal for students, commuters, travelers, and anyone who wants extra peace of mind, it’s a practical, discreet way to boost personal safety.

Design — Bug catcher

v2
PLA

A Bug Catcher is a simple, reusable bottle-top trap that fits common 16 oz and 2-liter soda bottles, turning them into an easy insect-catching container. Just attach the Bug Catcher to an empty bottle, bait it if needed, and place it where bugs are active—the insects enter through the top and are contained inside the bottle for safe viewing or release. Lightweight and portable, it’s a handy tool for home, garden, or outdoor use, and a fun, kid-friendly way to catch and observe insects without making a mess.

Design — Complex models

v3
Calibration

For testing complex prints like puzzles and intricate models, try interlocking puzzles, print-in-place hinges/gears, tiny text/detail tests, and snap-fit parts. These quickly reveal tolerance issues, overhang problems, and how well your printer handles fine features.
